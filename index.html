about me

Software engineer

I started my coding journey in June 2021 in the middle of the pandemic and I haven't stopped since. I fell in love with software development as it gave me the challenge to learn something new every single day. I can say that in the journey of mastering this skill, I have not only learned how to code problems, but also how to think of a solution or find the path to it. Hours and Hours that I put into thinking the solution to a problem makes the task more and more exciting and interesting, and moreover, knowing that I would reach to the solution of the problem motivates me to keep working on it. ...

Whether I am getting up to the final result or not doesn't haunt me much now as I know that I did give my full potential to it and as it is said, its about the journey and not the destination. Also seeing the face of failure is much much important before tasting the sweet of success because that makes you more inclined towards valuing both success and failures in your life, and also how to turn both of them into your favor by using them both learning lesson for future.
